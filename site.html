About Me

I am a 22 year old undergraduate at Portland State University currently in my 4th year of studies in the Computer Science program. I'm hoping to specialize in data analysis and working with large amounts of data, but I enjoy several other aspects of the field including cybersecurity and software development.

I found my passion for coding in middle school, when some of my friends convinced me to start learning how to code. It wasn't until high school that I started working on personal projects and decided it was what I wanted to study in University.

Outside of computer science, my hobbies include statistical analysis, skateboarding, and motorcycling. I've always enjoyed problem solving, whether it's finishing a project, learning a new skate trick, or finishing a difficult trail on my dirt bike. Breaking down and overcoming a problem is the greatest sense of satisfaction to me.
